I compose music and design sounds for a variety of needs, videogames, films, television, theatre, live performances, and radio.

I was born and raised in the "Musical capital" of Colombia surrounded by musicians, always have been fascinated by sounds, how they are produced, the effect of sounds on people, and how organizing them in a certain way result in a unique experience.


I've studied different forms of music to understand what is it that makes people react to it and as we know a product accompanied by catchy/memorable music could make a stronger emotional impact that can last longer in their memory.
